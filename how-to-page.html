How To Play


General rules: The game consists of two players. Each player is given a short summary of the situation and their needs and players must take turns typing their answers. The main objective of the game is for both players to reach a conclusion.

  1. Player 1 is given a minute to type their bargaining chips(Things they are willing to offer or give up to have their needs met.) and walk-away options (What they will do when no compromise can be reached). Once player 1 is done providing their input, the continue button should be clicked.
  2. Next, player 2 is given a minute to type their bargaining chips and walk-away options. Once player 2 is done providing their input, the continue button should be clicked.
  3. Player 1 and 2 are then given a minute to discuss and negotiate among themselves in an attempt to reach a conclusion. If both players can reach a conclusion, click the finalized decision button. Else if no conclusion can be reached, a player may choose to walk away by clicking the walk-away button.
